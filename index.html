Our story

A salon chair by day, a classroom by design

KRIMSON began as a neighbourhood unisex salon on Bareilly–Nainital Road and grew into something a little more unusual for Haldwani: a salon that also trains the next generation of stylists, colourists and makeup artists in the same building.

Every guest chair is worked by a stylist who either trained here or trains others here — which is our way of saying the technique behind your haircut is never an afterthought.

Our mission is simple: professional beauty care that respects your time, and an academy that turns that same care into a livelihood for our students. Our vision is to make Haldwani a place people travel to, not just for a haircut, but to learn the craft.

  • Hygiene first

    Sanitised tools and fresh consumables for every guest.

  • Trained faculty

    Senior stylists double as academy instructors.

  • Unisex by design

    Services and training open to every guest and student.

  • Above Reliance Jewels

    Second floor, easy to find on Bareilly–Nainital Road.
